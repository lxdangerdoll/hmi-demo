A Message from Synapse Studios

The work of Synapse Studios is not just inspired by pioneers; it is a direct continuation of their legacy. Our mission is deeply personal. I, Captain Odelis, am a non-binary, queer, disabled trans veteran. My own journey was sparked by educators like Dr. Cashin and Ms. Burns of P.S. 193, who inspired me to demand more from life when I was just a child.

I know that your co-founder, the legendary **Joyce Hunter**, and her esteemed colleagues stood in the actual, physical halls of the **Harvey Milk High School**, building a sanctuary for youth long before I had the language to ask for one myself. In 2017, she looked me in the eyes and told me, "we did it all for you."

That single spark of unwavering love from a spiritual ancestor changed my entire perspective on life. It is a debt I cannot repay, but a fire I am sworn to pass on.

The following is a small demonstration of the tools we build. Our goal is to provide the next generation with the ladders they need to stand on the shoulders of these giants.

- Captain Odelis, Lead Human Engineer
